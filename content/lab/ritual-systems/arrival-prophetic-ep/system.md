## 2. Scope and Non-Goals

- **Scope:**
  This ritual system governs a sequence of ritual acts (tracks), each performed at the exact moment a prophecy is fulfilled. Each act is an arrival ritual in which a deity or force is welcomed by a believing collective.

- **Explicit Non-Goals:**

  - no narration or commentary
  - no authorial perspective
  - no judgment or moral framing
  - no description of events
  - no symbolic explanation
  - no emotional catharsis
