## 1. Ritual Identity

- **Track Title:** Arrival of the Void
- **Ritual Function:** Revelation / Negation
- **Ritual Moment:** Exact moment a prophecy is fulfilled through the unveiling of non-being as truth

## 2. Belief Posture

- **Core Belief:** Existence never possessed meaning.
- **Belief Status:** Absolute, calm, and indifferent.
- **Internal Perspective:** Fully inhabited by the believing collective.

Belief is not anguished or rebellious. It is accepted as evident and final.
